About Us

Empowering Women and Families to Break the Cycle of Violence

The Shield of Athena provides emergency shelter, professional support, and culturally adapted services to women and children affected by family violence across Montreal and Laval.

Our Mission

The Shield is a charitable organization offering education, professional support, intervention and prevention services that are culturally and linguistically adapted to meet the needs of women, their children and ethnocultural communities.

We provide multilingual services in 16 languages, ensuring that every woman can access help in her language of origin. Our approach is rooted in respect, empowerment, and cultural sensitivity.

Through our offices in Montreal and Laval, our emergency shelter Athena's House, and our community outreach program, we offer comprehensive support to break the cycle of violence.
